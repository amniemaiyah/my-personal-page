Korean Food

Such as Ramen, Tteokbokki, Onigiri

Fun Fact about Korean Food

  • Did you know that ramen originated from China but was perfected in Japan and became a staple in Korean cuisine?
  • Tteokbokki, a popular Korean street food, was once a royal dish before it became a favorite snack!
  • Onigiri, a simple rice ball, has been a part of Japanese culture for centuries and is perfect for a quick meal or snack.

Sushi

Fun Fact about Sushi

  • Sushi was originally created as a form of preservation, where fish was fermented in rice.
  • In Japan, sushi chefs often train for years to perfect the craft, even learning how to handle rice properly.
  • The word "sushi" actually refers to the rice, not the fish!

Teh Ais & Coffee

Fun Fact about Teh Ais & Coffee

  • Teh Ais, a sweetened iced tea, is a popular drink in Malaysia and is often paired with local snacks.
  • Coffee is one of the most widely consumed beverages worldwide, with millions of cups drunk every day!
  • Did you know that the first coffeehouse opened in Istanbul in the 16th century?
